ABOUT

I train. I dress how I want. I'm building something.

Five days a week in the gym. Guard up before the bell, not during. The discipline isn't punishment — it's the only thing that feels like honesty.

The clothes came later, or maybe they were always there. Black everything. Layered. Intentional. Not fashion as a statement, just fashion as an extension of how I move through the world.

Guangzhou. Twenty-something. Figuring it out through what I wear and how I fight.
